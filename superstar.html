This is a weekly content, showcasing a particular talent with its SUPERSTAR. This week's talent is DANCE.



Karan Arora

Age - 20 yrs

Dance, the expression, Dance, the movement, Dance, the soul.
Dance, that flow with music, Dance, that evolve with the lyrics, Dance, that rest upon the energy.
Dance that holds the magic, Dance that believe in the power, Dance that is the passion.

Dance is the energy that awakes the nation, the term that is defined not by words but the performance. It is more about the stage than the script. The superstar this week is the man of the stage, the performer that express the lyrics. Karan Arora, 20 years, a student who works on revamping its energy for the audience. He perform for every himself as well as the world. He wants to touch the self of every being via his dance.

He has been practicing dance from numerous years, and his regular dance styles include Free style and Hip-Hop. He practice Slow motion Hip-Hop so that he could express the emotion more clearly. At the same time he believes that Dance is a never ending and more versatile process.

He has a different perception about perfection, perfection that more reflects the ideal state. Rather that reaching the ideal space, he is waiting for his audience, the folks that respect his stage, respect his passion. Yes, the stage is his passion, the movement is his journey.

The man is a learner, The Dance is his confidence.
