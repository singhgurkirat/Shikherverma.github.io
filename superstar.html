This is a weekly content, showcasing a particular talent with its SUPERSTAR. This week's talent is KINDNESS.


PRAVEEN BHYAN

Age - 24 yrs

When faith is a slave of humanity,
When the being opt for wisdom,
When kindness is the need of the hour.
When the artist holds compassion,
When the being follows the emotion,
When the man practice introspection.

This week’s talent is kindness and our superstar, we believe holds this talent. But, for him,” I would choose to be wise rather than being kind and yes, I have compassion for fellow beings.” He believes that it supports growth of civilization.

Life is entitled as a beautiful journey by the saints but now it is left as a racing track where every individual is working to climb success. So, nowadays practicing kindness is a difficult task. But, since it is an emotion, it drives the sequence of events and acts as catalyst in some situations. And, our superstar narrates it as an intangible force that should be learnt. He adds on by defining kindness as understanding the nuances of need and desire of the person and thereby behaving or responding accordingly.

Our superstar is one of us, a student, a son, a being, a man that believes in not just the inside self but respects the self of every being. The artist that neither spread colors nor the flow of music, but a man that spread love, the being that cherish humanity.
