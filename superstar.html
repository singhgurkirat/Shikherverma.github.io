This is a weekly content, showcasing a particular talent with its SUPERSTAR. This week's talent is DANCE.



Bhavya Jaitley

Age - 21 yrs

The talent that lies in the taste,
The beauty that lies in the fragrance,
The art that holds joy…

This week’s superstar is not less than an artist, the man that performs for the taste, the ideal that dresses the veggies. For the man who cooks, it is defined as an entertainment package, the art, the passion that works as a hobby and make him happy. From years it grew up to become a part of his life. Beginning from bread omelet to the recipes of chicken, cooking for this artist is his inspiration that completes him; it is his satisfaction that inspires him.

When the artist begun, to cook was his requirement, the only need for the hour, but slowly and gradually it became a part of the self. His first attempt though wasn’t perfect, the omelet ended up as a vegetable shower but that was his beginning to a never ending journey.

The most beautiful part of this art lies in the taste, the flavor that is always new. Every time you dress the veggies, it narrates a new flavor. This week’s superstar, our cook believes that he’ll always be a learner, awaited to work as a chef that cook for the world, the art that is incomplete without its audience.
